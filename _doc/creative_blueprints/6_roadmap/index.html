What's Next

Next-Phase Capabilities

Three concrete product goals that extend the MVP into a fully operational, multi-site warehouse platform.

Goal 1

Mobile Barcode Scanner & Quick Check-In

Employees scan barcodes with a phone camera to instantly pull up item location in the 3D map and update stock quantities on the warehouse floor — no desktop required.

  • ✓Camera-based barcode & QR scan
  • ✓Mobile-optimized 3D location view
  • ✓Inline quantity update & check-in confirmation
  • ✓Movement history entry on scan
Goal 2

Advanced Analytics & Reporting Center

A strategic reporting layer for managers: velocity, aging, trends, reorder automation, and exportable reports — turning the dashboard into a decision-support tool.

  • ✓Stock movement velocity & inventory aging
  • ✓Category-level trend analysis
  • ✓Reorder point automation
  • ✓Exportable PDF & CSV reports
Goal 3

Multi-Warehouse Network & Transfer Management

Manage inventory across multiple warehouse locations in a single account. Initiate transfers between sites and track items in transit until receipt is confirmed.

  • ✓Site-level location hierarchy
  • ✓Cross-site stock transfer initiation
  • ✓In-transit item tracking
  • ✓Receipt confirmation at destination
