Foundation Validated

Warehouse inefficiency and time wasted searching for inventory

The Problem

Manual inventory searches consume hours daily. Warehouse employees navigate aisles without guidance, managers lack real-time stock visibility, and layout understanding is fragmented. Inefficiency cascades into lost time, misplaced stock, and operational blind spots.

The Solution

Manual inventory searches waste hours daily. Employees navigate aisles blindly, managers lack real-time visibility into stock levels and storage utilization. Without a spatial understanding of warehouse layout, locating items is slow, error-prone, and costly.

Target Audience

Warehouse employees and managers operating mid-to-large distribution centers who need faster item location and real-time inventory visibility

Personal Motivation

Operational precision and speed are competitive advantages. A spatial 3D interface reduces search time by orders of magnitude while keeping all stakeholders aligned on actual inventory state.
