Why It Matters

The Problem & The Solution

The Problem

Warehouses grow. Finding items shouldn't be a hunt.

  • ✕ Employees spend minutes walking the floor searching for a single item
  • ✕ Spreadsheets and paper maps collapse under real warehouse workload
  • ✕ Stale location data — records never match actual stock on the floor
  • ✕ Low-stock items go unnoticed until operations are already disrupted
The Solution

A live 3D warehouse that shows exactly where everything is.

  • ✓ Search by name, SKU, or barcode — item glows on the 3D map instantly
  • ✓ Zone → Rack → Shelf → Bin hierarchy with 3D path navigation from the entrance
  • ✓ Quantities and locations sync in real time as movements are recorded
  • ✓ Automatic low-stock alerts fire before shortages disrupt operations
